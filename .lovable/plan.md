## ðŸš€ Core Features

### 1. User Authentication & Onboarding
- Sign up / Login with email or social accounts
- Multi-step onboarding wizard collecting:
  - **Basic metrics:** Age, gender, height, weight, body type
  - **Diet info:** Eating habits, restrictions, allergies
  - **Fitness history:** Activity level, workout experience, injuries
  - **Lifestyle factors:** Sleep patterns, work schedule, stress levels
- BMI & BMR calculation displayed immediately

### 2. Card-Based Dashboard
- **Today's Overview:** Key metrics at a glance
- **Quick Stats:** BMI, BMR, daily calorie goal
- **Progress Cards:** Weight trend, workout streak, posture score
- **Action Cards:** Today's workout, meals, and tasks
- **Motivational elements:** Streak counters, achievements

### 3. Posture Detection & Improvement
- **Self-Assessment Mode:** Interactive questionnaire about sitting/standing habits with personalized tips
- **Camera-Based AI Analysis:** Real-time posture check using device camera with AI feedback
- **Posture Score:** Track improvements over time
- **Correction Exercises:** Video-guided exercises for common issues

### 4. AI-Powered Personalized Plans
Using AI to generate customized recommendations:
- **Diet Plans:** Meal suggestions based on goals, preferences, and restrictions
- **Workout Routines:** Exercise programs tailored to fitness level and goals
- **Sleep Optimization:** Bedtime/wake recommendations based on lifestyle
- **Daily To-Do List:** Prioritized health tasks for the day

### 5. Progress Tracking
- **Body Metrics Charts:** Weight, measurements, BMI trends over time
- **Workout Logs:** Record exercises, sets, reps, and duration
- **Meal Tracking:** Log daily food intake against plan
- **Posture Progress:** Score history and improvement timeline

### 6. Profile & Settings
- Edit personal information and goals
- Adjust dietary preferences
- Notification settings
- Dark/light theme toggle (defaulting to dark)
