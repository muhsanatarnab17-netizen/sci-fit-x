# POSFITX: MASTER ARCHITECTURAL BLUEPRINT

## 🎯 The Vision
A premium, commercial-grade AI Health Ecosystem. Designed for high-end UI/UX, user retention, and future monetization (affiliate/e-commerce).

## 🚀 Core Features & Future Scalability
1. **AI Posture Engine:** Live "Tri-Metric" analysis (CVA, Shoulder, Symmetry).
2. **AI Health Plans:** Dynamic workout/nutrition generation.
3. **Content Engine (Future):** Support for workout videos, meal GIFs, and task-based animations.
4. **Marketplace Integration (Future):** UI areas dedicated to promoting fitness accessories and health products.
5. **Full Dashboard:** Vitals tracking and historical progress.

## 🎨 Visual Identity (Biotech-Commercial)
- **Aesthetic:** "Dark Biotech" / Luxury Cyber-Medical.
- **Backgrounds:** Deep layered dark gradients with subtle motion.
- **Typography:** High-readability, modern sans-serif (Inter/Geist) with monospace accents for tech metrics.
- **Components:** - **Buttons:** Neon-bordered glassmorphism with "Glow-on-hover" effects.
  - **Animations:** Smooth Framer Motion transitions and "Scanning" laser effects.
  - **Media:** Optimized containers for high-res workout images and video embeds.

## ⚙️ Technical Guardrails
- **Stack:** Vite, React, TS, Tailwind, Supabase (Deno), MediaPipe.
- **Data Architecture:** Ensure PostgreSQL tables are ready to handle media links and product metadata.
